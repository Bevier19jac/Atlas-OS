# Atlas OS Product Brief

## What Atlas OS Is

Atlas OS is an AI-powered operating system for ambitious entrepreneurs. It helps founders turn scattered ideas into executable businesses through strategy, automation, and disciplined execution.

## Who It Serves

Atlas OS serves entrepreneurs, operators, and service founders who have raw ideas or early traction but need sharper positioning, a clearer first offer, and a practical execution path.

## First Paid Offer

The first paid offer is the Atlas Idea-to-Business Sprint. It is a productized strategy sprint that turns a messy idea into a monetizable business blueprint.

## MVP Purpose

The Sprint 1 MVP creates the technical foundation for the first offer. It is not the full Atlas OS platform. The goal is to establish clean app structure, routes, reusable components, mock data, and product documentation.

## What The MVP Does

- Explains Atlas OS and the Idea-to-Business Sprint on a landing page.
- Collects a founder's raw idea through a local intake form.
- Shows a mock client dashboard for future sprint workspaces.
- Displays a complete sample business blueprint.
- Uses reusable components, shared TypeScript types, and mock data.

## What The MVP Does Not Do Yet

- No authentication.
- No payments.
- No production database.
- No AI API calls.
- No automated blueprint generation.
- No real client account management.

## Long-Term Product Vision

Atlas OS can evolve from a manual productized service into a subscription platform where entrepreneurs manage business strategy, generate blueprints, track execution, discover automation opportunities, and access templates or workflows that help them build recurring revenue.
